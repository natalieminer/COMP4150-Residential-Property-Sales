Our Movtivation

The retail industry now heavily relies on data analytics to gain insights into consumer behaviour and preferences. This project focuses on analyzing the sales of house properties in a city in Australia to determine the key factors that influence the sales prices of these properties. The dataset contains several variables such as date sold, postcode, price, and bedrooms etc. by examining these variables and assessing their relationships with one another, we can uncover insights that provide valuable knowledge to real estate agents and property owners alike. Understanding the factors that influence property value can help property owners determine the best time and trends followed to sell their property and make any necessary renovations that may increase its value. On the other hand, property buyers can see the insights to determine which areas align with their interests and requirements. It can also help real estate agents to provide more accurate and personalized pricing recommendations to their clients. Overall, this project will provide meaningful insights into the real estate market and help improve the profitability and efficiency of the industry through data analytics.
